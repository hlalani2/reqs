Reqs Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Reqs is for sharing genuine recommendations with people who actually know you. Keep it honest, keep it helpful, keep it kind. These guidelines apply to everything you post: the recommendation itself, the "why," details, disclosures, deal notes, and your display name.

Not allowed

  • Hate speech and slurs of any kind, including content that demeans people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality.
  • Harassment, bullying, or threats directed at anyone, on or off the app.
  • Profanity and sexually explicit content. Reqs is used by families and friends — keep posts clean.
  • Violence or dangerous content, including glorifying or encouraging harm.
  • Illegal goods and services, including drugs and weapons.
  • Spam and scams: repetitive posting, pyramid schemes, deceptive offers, or links designed to mislead.
  • Undisclosed self-promotion. If you own it, work there, sell it, or benefit from it, you must say so using the disclosure feature.
  • Impersonation or posting someone else's personal information.

How we enforce this

  • Automatic filtering. Every post is checked before it goes out. Posts containing profanity or slurs are rejected on the spot.
  • Reporting. Every recommendation has a Report option (tap ⋯ on the post). Reported content is immediately hidden from your feed, and every report is reviewed within 24 hours.
  • Removal and bans. Content that breaks these guidelines is deleted, and accounts that repeatedly or seriously violate them are terminated. There is zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users.
  • Your controls. You can mute or block any person at any time from the People tab or from any of their posts — their content disappears from your feed instantly.

Report anything

If you see something that shouldn't be on Reqs, report it in the app or email hlalani@lalanirxconsulting.com. We act on every report within 24 hours.
